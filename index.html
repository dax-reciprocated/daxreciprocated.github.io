About DAX Reciprocated

After serving as a Veterans Service Officer, I saw many veterans struggle to understand their VA decision letters and the rating criteria used by the Department of Veterans Affairs.

DAX Reciprocated provides educational consultations that help veterans better understand how the VA evaluates evidence, how disability ratings are calculated, and what questions they may want to ask when working with an accredited representative.

Veterans sometimes choose to voluntarily share their VA decision letters or other documents during a consultation so we can review them together for educational purposes. You remain in control of what information you share.

DAX Reciprocated LLC provides educational information only. I am a former Veterans Service Officer but I am not acting as an accredited representative, attorney, or claims agent. I do not prepare, submit, or represent veterans in VA claims or appeals. I am not affiliated with the U.S. Department of Veterans Affairs or any veterans service organization.
