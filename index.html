About DAX Reciprocated

After serving as a Veterans Service Officer, I witnessed too many veterans leave benefits on the table due to missing nexus letters, incomplete forms, or rating calculation errors. DAX Reciprocated LLC was built to give veterans clear education about their decisions and options—one claim at a time.

Mission: Ensure every veteran receives the disability compensation they earned through faithful service.

DAX Reciprocated LLC provides educational information to help veterans better understand their VA disability decisions and options. I am a former Veterans Service Officer, not an attorney. I do not provide legal advice or legal representation, and I am not affiliated with the Department of Veterans Affairs or any veterans service organization. Any information you choose to share with me is provided voluntarily so we can review it together for educational purposes only.
